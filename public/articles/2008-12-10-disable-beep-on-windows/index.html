Disable Beep on Windows

Just switched the team/room and it’s, oh, so quiet here. Even replaced my keyboard because the keys were too loud. No Kidding!

  • open regedit
  • find HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Control Panel\Sound
  • create or edit the Key Beep and set it to no
  • silence!
